01 / About

The infrastructure layer between architecture and execution.

Teams call me when the deploy pipeline breaks. When a cluster is on fire. When someone needs a new AWS environment by Thursday. I design systems at the architecture level. I also do the ground-floor work of unblocking developers one environment at a time.
